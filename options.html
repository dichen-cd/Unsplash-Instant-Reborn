How to get your Unsplash API Key:

  1. Go to the Unsplash Developers website.
  2. Click "Join" or "Log in" to create an account or sign in.
  3. Once logged in, click "Your apps" (or similar) and then "New Application".
  4. Agree to the API Guidelines.
  5. Give your application a name (e.g., "My Chrome New Tab Extension") and a description.
  6. Once your application is created, you will see your **"Access Key"**. This is your API key!
  7. Copy and paste the **Access Key** into the field below.
